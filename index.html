How to use Word Search
Basics
🖱️Click any word to copy it to your clipboard instantly.
📋Paste or drop a .txt file into the word list box to load your words.
Modes
🖤Black Tea — search for individual words by typing any substring.
❤️Red Tea — finds the longest word(s) in your list, optionally filtered by a substring.
💛Yellow Tea — picks a batch of matching words at once; set the count on the right.
Controls
🗑️Remove on copy — word disappears from results after you copy it.
↻Refresh — brings back all removed words (or press Esc).
⚡Auto-copy — automatically copies the best match once you've typed 3+ letters.
⎚Auto-clear — clears the search bar immediately after a word is copied.
⇄Sep — (Yellow Tea only) changes how the copied batch is joined: Space, Comma, or New Line. Click it to cycle through the three.
Preset Dictionaries
📍You can find the preset dictionary buttons at the very top of the page.
📗CSW24 — Collins Scrabble Words 2024, the standard for international Scrabble.
📘NWL2023 — NASPA Word List 2023 Edition, the standard for North American Scrabble.
📙SOWPODS — A comprehensive combined word list widely used in competitive English Scrabble.
📒English™ — Just English words™.
⚠️The Load Dictionary button loads a general-purpose dictionary that may be less complete than the preset lists above.
About
🀄Built mainly for Mudae's $tea word game and similar word-guessing games, where you need to quickly find a word containing a specific letter combo — but it works just as well for Scrabble, Wordle helpers, or any other word game.
