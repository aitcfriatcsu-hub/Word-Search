How to use Word Search
Basics
🖱️Click any word to copy it to your clipboard instantly.
📋Paste or drop a .txt file into the word list box to load your words.
Modes
🖤Black Tea — search for individual words by typing any substring.
❤️Red Tea — finds the longest word(s) in your list, optionally filtered by a substring.
💛Yellow Tea — picks a batch of matching words at once; set the count on the right.
Controls
🗑️Remove on copy — word disappears from results after you copy it.
↻Refresh — brings back all removed words (or press Esc).
⚡Auto-copy — automatically copies the best match once you've typed 3+ letters.
⎚Auto-clear — clears the search bar immediately after a word is copied.
⇄Sep — (Yellow Tea only) changes how the copied batch is joined: Space, Comma, or New Line. Click it to cycle through the three.
Preset Dictionaries
📍The preset dictionary buttons sit beside the Load Dictionary button at the bottom of the word list — click any to load it instantly.
📗CSW24 — Collins Scrabble Words 2024, the standard for UK and international Scrabble.
📘NWL2023 — NASPA Word List 2023 Edition, the standard for North American Scrabble.
📙SOWPODS — A comprehensive combined word list widely used in competitive English Scrabble.
📒English™ — Just English words™.
⚠️Load Dictionary loads a general-purpose dictionary. Once loaded, a Reset Word List button appears below it to restore your original list.
Basics
🟩The Wordle Solver helps you narrow down possible words based on your guesses.
⌨️Type a letter into each cell, then click the cell to cycle its color: grey → yellow → green.
Colors
⬛Grey — that letter is not in the word at all.
🟨Yellow — the letter is in the word, but not at that position.
🟩Green — the letter is in the word at exactly that position.
Controls
➕Add Row — add another past guess to refine the results further.
🔍Solve — filters the dictionary and shows all words still possible (or press Enter in any cell).
↺Reset — clears all guesses and results to start fresh.
🖱️Click any result word to copy it to your clipboard instantly.
Tips
💡The dictionary loads automatically when you switch to Wordle mode — wait for the badge to show a word count before solving.
🔤Duplicate letters work correctly: if you marked a letter grey and yellow, only the yellow rule applies.
Pattern input
🔡Type known letters in their exact positions. Use _ or ? for unknown letters.
✏️C_O_S_W_R_ matches CROSSWORD.
✏️__TION matches any 6-letter word ending in TION.
✏️?AT matches BAT, CAT, HAT, MAT, RAT…
Letter constraints
✅Contains letters — the word must include all letters you list (comma or space separated). E.g. A, R
❌Excludes letters — the word must not contain any of the letters you list. E.g. Z, Q
💡Combine a pattern and letter constraints together for very precise results.
Basics
📖Before searching, load a dictionary using the buttons at the top of the Scrabble section — CSW24, NWL2023, or SOWPODS.
🔠Enter up to 7 rack letters and the helper finds every valid word you can make from them, scored using official Scrabble letter values.
❓Use ? for a blank tile — it matches any letter (scores 0 points).
🖱️Click any result word to copy it to your clipboard (when the board is hidden) or to select it for placement (when the board is visible).
Filters
✅Must use letters — only show words that use all the letters you list (comma or space separated).
📏Word length — filter by exact length (e.g. 5) or a range (e.g. 4-7).
Sorting
⭐Score — sorts highest-scoring words first (default).
📏Length — sorts longest words first.
🔤A–Z — sorts alphabetically.
🎲 Interactive Board
🎲Click Show Board to open the 15×15 Scrabble board with all premium squares (TWS, DWS, TLS, DLS) in their correct positions.
🖱️Place words from results: With the board open, click any result word to select it. A highlighted Placing panel appears at the top — choose → Across or ↓ Down, then click any board cell to place it. Hover cells to preview the score first.
⌨️Type any word: The box above the board labelled "Type any word" lets you place a word of any length, with no 7-letter cap — type it, click ✓ Use word, then click a cell (or type a coordinate) to place it just like a result word. This is the easiest way to enter an opponent's word, especially a longer one they made by building off letters already on the board.
⇄Flip direction: Click the ⇄ Flip button (or click the direction buttons) at any time before placing to instantly switch between Across and Down without re-selecting the word.
⌨️Type individual letters: Click any empty cell on the board to open a letter input. Type a letter and click Place to set it manually — useful for typing in opponent tiles or board state. Click a manually-placed tile to edit or remove it.
🧩Board-aware results & hints: Once the board has tiles on it, both the word list and the 💡 Hint button also search for words that thread through letters already on the board — so they can suggest words longer than your rack alone could spell (e.g. rack GGTICLY + board letters spelling GIGANTICALLY). These results are marked with a 🧩 board badge and show their real on-board score, including cross-words.
❓Blank tiles on the board: In the letter input, type ?A (a ? followed by the letter) to place a blank tile showing that letter — it scores 0 points but stands out with a dark red letter on a yellow tile, so it's easy to spot at a glance. You can also click an existing tile and use the ⇄ Make Blank / Make Normal button to toggle it.
🟨Blank tile appearance: The 🟨 Blank: Yellow button in the board header toggles how blank tiles look. 🟨 Yellow makes blank tiles look similar to normal tiles (amber, 0 pts). ⬜ Normal colours them the same as the board background, making them more visually distinct.
💯Score preview: Hover over a board cell while a word is selected to see the estimated score shown in the placement panel. Each word tile in the results also shows a green score badge with its potential points once you hover a valid placement cell.
🗑️Remove tiles: Click any placed word-tile to remove that entire word from the board. Click a manually-placed tile to edit or delete just that letter.
🧮The Total score counter in the board header adds up the scores of all placed words, accounting for all premium squares.
🔡 Auto-Deduct
🔡Toggle Auto-Deduct in the board header to automatically remove used letters from your rack whenever you place a word on the board.
✅Auto-Deduct: On — after placing a word, those letters are instantly deducted from your rack and the results refresh to show only words you can still play with your remaining tiles.
↩️Removing a placed word — if you click a word-tile to remove it from the board while Auto-Deduct is on, those letters are automatically returned to your rack.
💡Turn Auto-Deduct off if you want to keep your full rack visible regardless of what's already placed — useful when planning multiple turns ahead.
🔄 Turn Mode
🔄Toggle Turn Mode in the board header to track your score and your opponent's score separately as you both play, instead of one combined total.
👤A Current turn indicator appears — switch between 👤 My Turn and 🆚 Opponent's Turn before placing a word, so it's credited to the right player. The turn switches automatically after each word is placed.
⌨️To enter what your opponent played, switch to 🆚 Opponent's Turn, then use the Type any word box (or a result word) to place it — this works for words of any length, including ones longer than 7 letters built off the existing board.
↺Reset Scores sets both totals back to 0 without clearing the board.
